About Me

With a Ph.D. in Fluid Mechanics and over six years of experience in computational and experimental fluid dynamics, I specialize in CFD, thermal analysis, multiphase flows, conjugate heat transfer (CHT), and discrete phase modeling. Currently, I lead technical CFD projects at SLB (Schlumberger), focusing on fluid analysis, erosion mitigation, and injection molding simulation. I am deeply committed to developing innovative and efficient solutions to complex fluid dynamics problems.
